How to Track Your Steps

  1. Get a pedometer or use your phones health app.
  2. Set a daily step goal (e.g., 10,000 steps).
  3. Walk as much as you can throughout the day.
  4. Track progress by checking your pedometer or app regularly.
  5. Celebrate when you reach your goal!
